Sales Enablement

Why an Enterprise Prospect Might Pass

Three realistic objections to Merge Agent Handler — and the underlying concerns driving each one.

01

Existing integration infrastructure

Many large enterprises already run an iPaaS (MuleSoft, Workato, Boomi) or an internal integrations platform with years of investment. Adopting Merge duplicates that stack and forces a build-vs-buy conversation they've already settled.

Build vs. Buy
02

Data governance and third-party processing concerns

Routing customer data through an external service triggers InfoSec review around data residency, multi-tenancy isolation, compliance scope (SOC 2 / HIPAA / FedRAMP / GDPR), and breach liability. For regulated industries or zero-trust shops, one more vendor in the data path is a hard sell.

Security & Compliance
03

Coverage gaps for legacy or proprietary systems

"Thousands of connectors" covers modern SaaS well, but enterprises often live on mainframe systems, internal APIs, regional tools, or acquired-company stacks that won't be in the catalog. If the mission-critical integrations still need custom work, the value of the platform shrinks.
